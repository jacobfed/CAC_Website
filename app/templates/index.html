Welcome to Canopy Child Advocacy Center of Cleveland

A CAC is a national best practice model utilized to reduce potential trauma to children and families impacted by child abuse and improve services through interagency collaboration. Clients come to one child-friendly, trauma-informed location where they will receive a wide array of services to help in both the short and long term healing process.
