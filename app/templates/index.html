Welcome to Canopy Child Advocacy Center

A Child Advocacy Center (CAC) is a national best practice model utilized to reduce potential trauma to children and families impacted by child abuse and improve services through interagency collaboration. Clients come to one child-friendly, trauma-informed location where they will receive a wide array of services to help in both the short and long term healing process.

Learn more HERE

Mission

Our mission is to serve children and families affected by child abuse through a multi-disciplinary partnership focused on safety, healing, and well-being.

Vision

We envision a community where children are safe, families are resilient, and those affected by child abuse are empowered to heal and thrive.
