Open House and Ribbon Cutting Ceremony

Thank you to each of you for your support of the Canopy Child Advocacy Center. We’re so grateful to each that were able to attend our open house and ribbon cutting ceremony on November 14th! Thank you each for taking time to recognize this significant milestone reached within our county! The center staff and community partners highly value each of you, and look forward to your continued support of Canopy. If by chance, you were not able to attend the open house and desire a tour of the center, please feel free to reach out to Jennifer Johnson, Director, at jjohnson@dvcac.org and she will happily arrange a tour at your earliest convenience.
